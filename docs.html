Getting Started

VibeCaaS makes it easy to deploy containerized applications with just a few clicks. Follow these steps to get started:

1. Create Your Account

Sign up for a free VibeCaaS account to access the platform. You'll get 3 free deployments to start with.

2. Connect Your GitHub

Link your GitHub account to deploy directly from your repositories. VibeCaaS will automatically detect your Dockerfile and configuration.

3. Deploy Your First Container

Select a repository and click "Deploy". Our AI assistant will help you configure the optimal settings.

# Example: Deploy from GitHub
$ vibecaas deploy --repo username/repository
$ vibecaas status deployment-id
$ vibecaas logs deployment-id

Container Management

Manage your containers efficiently with our comprehensive tools:

Container Lifecycle

Control every aspect of your container's lifecycle:

# Container commands
$ vibecaas start container-id
$ vibecaas stop container-id
$ vibecaas restart container-id
$ vibecaas scale container-id --replicas 3

Version Control

Track changes and rollback deployments when needed. VibeCaaS maintains a complete history of your deployments.

Pro Tip: Enable automatic rollback on failed health checks to maintain high availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is VibeCaaS?

VibeCaaS is a Container as a Service platform that simplifies deploying and managing containerized applications with AI-powered assistance.

How does pricing work?

We offer a free tier with 3 deployments. Professional plans start at $49/month for unlimited deployments. Check our pricing page for details.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! Professional and Enterprise plans support custom domains with automatic SSL certificates.

What cloud infrastructure do you use?

VibeCaaS runs on NVIDIA Cloud infrastructure, providing high-performance computing with GPU support for AI workloads.
